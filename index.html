Ouroboros Platform

A modular Symfony core for business management, extensible via modules, hooks, and an open marketplace.

View on GitHub

A solid core, infinite possibilities

Ouroboros Platform provides a ready-to-use Symfony core, designed for business management (CRM, HR, invoicing, purchasing, etc.). With its modular and extensible architecture, you can:

  • Use official modules (CRM, HR, etc.).
  • Extend each module via hooks and Symfony events.
  • Create your own modules and share them on the marketplace.
  • Customize the appearance with themes.

Ouroboros is open source: you control everything, without relying on proprietary solutions.

Available Modules

Ouroboros Platform includes ready-to-use modules, designed to cover common business needs.

📊 CRM

Customer and prospect management, sales pipelines.

👥 Human Resources

Employee management, leave tracking, payroll.

💰 Invoicing

Invoice and quote creation, payment tracking.

🛒 Purchasing

Supplier management, orders, and inventory.

📅 Projects

Task and team management, deadlines.

⚙️ Settings

Global configuration and customization.

Infinitely Extensible

Ouroboros is designed to be 100% extensible:

🔌 Hooks & Events

Each module exposes hooks and Symfony events to extend or modify its behavior.

// Example: Listening to a CRM contact creation event $dispatcher->addListener( 'ouroboros.crm.contact_created', function(ContactEvent $event) { // Your custom logic here } );

📦 Marketplace

Share your modules with the community via the official marketplace, or use those developed by others.

🎨 Themes

Customize the platform's appearance with themes, or create your own using our templating system.
